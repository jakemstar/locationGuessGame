Welcome! This is my own take on the game Spyfall. Start by entering a username and the room you would like to join.

Rules
Upon the start of this game everyone, except one person (the spy), is given a location. Starting with the leader (the person who joined first) and going in an order that the players decide, each player takes a turn asking one other player one question with the goal of finding who is the spy. If a player suspects another player, they can raise a vote and if a majority of players agree the suspect is guilty, the suspect reveals whether or not they are the spy. If the suspect is not the spy, the spy wins. If the suspect is the spy the players win. If the spy can guess the location before being voted out, they win.

*note these rules are sugguested guidelines but play however you and others want to!
